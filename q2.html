Animal

1) lion

Lions are large, muscular cats belonging to the Felidae family.They are native to Africa and are known for their majestic appearance and distinctive manes, which are more prominent in males. Lions are social animals and often live in groups called prides, which typically consist of related females, their offspring, and a few adult males. They are carnivores and primarily hunt large ungulates such as zebras, wildebeests, and buffalo.

2) cat

Cats are small, carnivorous mammals belonging to the Felidae family and They are one of the most popular pets worldwide and have been domesticated for thousands of years. Cats come in various breeds, sizes, and coat patterns, and they exhibit a wide range of behaviors.Domestic cats are known for their agility, grace, and hunting instincts, which they use to catch small prey such as mice, birds, and insects.

3) tiger

Tigers are the largest cats in the world and belong to the Felidae family.They are native to various habitats across Asia, including forests, grasslands, and mangrove swamps. Tigers are solitary animals and typically establish large territories, which they mark with scent markings
